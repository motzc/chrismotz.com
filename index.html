About

Yours Truly

Hi there! My name is Chris and I'm a multidisciplinary design student pursuing a MA/MSc in Global Innovation Design at the Royal College of Art and Imperial College London. As part of this program, I’ve studied product design and design engineering through a number of projects on topics such as healthcare, personal information management, and independent work. Additionally, I spent 8 months living in Tokyo and Singapore to complement my studies by gaining a global perspective. Previously, I studied at The Wharton School at The University of Pennsylvania, graduating cum laude with concentrations in Finance and Marketing & Operations Management, as well as a minor in History.

I've had the pleasure of working at a few amazing companies: OrderIn, a Cape Town-based online food delivery service and Thumbtack, a local service marketplace based out of San Francisco. At both companies, I worked in growth marketing roles, helping them refine their customer acquisition efforts across various channels. At Thumbtack, I split my time with the brand team and led the initial production of their first TV commercial to feature a real customer.

On my spare time, I love to run, ski, and play guitar. I'm also trying to travel around the world as far as my appetite will take me. Please feel free to look through my prior work and projects. Don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to connect.
