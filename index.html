About

Yours Truly

Hi there! My name is Chris and I'm a multidisciplinary designer. I am a recent graduate in Global Innovation Design (MA/MSc) from the Royal College of Art and Imperial College London, where I studied product design and design engineering through a number of projects in topics such as healthcare, personal information management, and independent work. Additionally, I spent 8 months living in Tokyo and Singapore, where I studied at Keio University and Nanyang Technological University, respectively. Previously, I studied at The Wharton School at The University of Pennsylvania, graduating cum laude with concentrations in Finance and Marketing & Operations Management, as well as a minor in History.

I've had the pleasure of working at a few amazing companies: Nana, a marketplace that provides AI-enabled appliance repair services from Oakland; Thumbtack, a local services marketplace based out of San Francisco; and OrderIn, a Cape Town-based online food delivery service and. At each of these companies, I worked in various product design and growth marketing roles, helping them refine their customer acquisition efforts across various channels.

On my spare time, I love to run, ski, and play guitar. I'm also trying to travel around the world as far as my appetite will take me. Please feel free to look through my prior work and projects. Don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to connect.
