Thumbtack TV Commercial

Meet Oleg!

Have you ever needed to find a landscaper, handyman, or pool cleaner? Oleg did, but luckily, he was able to hire all of these professionals on Thumbtack.com, an online marketplace for local service professionals. While working on the marketing team for Thumbtack, I led initial production of their first TV commercial to feature a real customer. While speaking to customers, I met Thumbtack power user Oleg, who now stars in what is currently Thumbtack’s best performing TV commercial, ‘Meet Oleg’.

San Fracisco, CA | August 2017
